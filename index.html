Who am I?

I currently hold the position of software tester. I am responsible for both automatic and manual testing of one of the largest project photo documentation application in Europe. Previously, I held the position of Website Production Leader in one of the largest Internet consulting companies in Poland. I am a computer scientist by education - I graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science. My great interests include football, programming, military and automotive. I single out as my greatest strengths my ability to learn quickly and my determination to achieve my goals.

My final grade after this stage was "5". After completing my bachelor's degree, and I followed suit - I decided to pursue a full-time master's degree in "Computer Science". Joomla Webmaster

I started my career from the position of Joomla Webmaster in October 2019. My tasks included coding websites based on the prepared graphic design and based on RWD and SEO standards. The website production process was based on Joomla's proprietary CMS overlay. This position required me to have a good knowledge of typical WEB technologies such as Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, JavaScript. Also, on more than one occasion, I had to demonstrate my knowledge of the Gimp tool to perform image file processing.

Website production leader

After less than two years as a Joomla Webmaster, I was appointed as a Website Production Leader. This was a great honor and both a great responsibility for me. My main task was to coordinate a group of Webmasters. I faced many difficult and responsible tasks. I was responsible for all members of my Team. In doing so, I adhered to the principle of brotherhood - my people knew that they could turn to me with any issue. They also felt responsible for every activity they performed. It was a great lesson for me and for my character.

Software tester

In addition, I use tools such as testng, Allure reports, Selenium CDP and Rest assured. I am also responsible for manual testing of both web and mobile applications. I also perform performance tests using the JMeter tool. In the near future, I will also start creating automated tests for a mobile application based on Appium.
